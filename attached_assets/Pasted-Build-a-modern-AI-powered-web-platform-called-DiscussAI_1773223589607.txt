Build a modern, AI-powered web platform called “DiscussAI” that allows students to practice Group Discussion (GD) rounds for placements with AI participants.

The platform should simulate real placement GD environments, where multiple AI agents discuss a topic while the student participates. The system then provides detailed AI feedback on communication skills, clarity, leadership, and relevance.

The design must feel premium, futuristic, and highly interactive, with smooth animations and a visually impressive UI.

Core Features
1. AI Group Discussion Simulation

The platform must simulate 3–5 AI participants in a group discussion.

Each AI participant must have a distinct personality style:

Logical Thinker – structured arguments

Data Analyst – supports points with statistics

Aggressive Debater – interrupts and challenges ideas

Balanced Speaker – mediates and summarizes

Moderator – guides discussion and ends session

The student joins the discussion and can type or speak responses.

The AI participants should respond dynamically using an AI model.

Discussion Flow

User selects a GD topic

AI participants introduce themselves

Discussion starts

AI participants exchange opinions

Student joins and contributes

AI reacts to the student’s statements

After 5–7 minutes, moderator ends the discussion

System generates performance analysis

AI Feedback System

After the discussion, generate a detailed evaluation report based on:

Communication clarity

Relevance to topic

Leadership (initiating or summarizing)

Confidence (participation frequency)

Argument strength

Listening and response quality

Display scores out of 10 for each metric and provide improvement suggestions.

Example output:

Communication: 7.5 / 10
Clarity: 8 / 10
Leadership: 6 / 10

Suggestions:

Provide examples to strengthen arguments

Try summarizing discussion points at the end
